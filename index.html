HOW TO READ THIS MAP

The entire tournament as one transit system. Every national team is a line in its own colors; every match is a station where two lines meet. Win and your service continues. Lose and it's the end of the line.

A team's line — hover to isolate its full route, click for the journey receipt.
Group-stage matchday results: win, draw, loss.
Terminus bar — the service ended here. The label is the score that ended it.
Match station — the core is the winner's color. Click for full match details.
Under construction — the route exists, the traveler doesn't. Hover a future station to see every team that can still reach it.
The Bronze Shuttle — semifinal losers' spur to the third-place match.
The crown terminal at MetLife Stadium — reachable by exactly one line.

The left third is the group stage: twelve districts, four local lines each. The 24 lines that don't qualify terminate at the district gate. The 32 that do fan through the sorting yard into the knockout trunk, where every station halves the network until one line remains.
