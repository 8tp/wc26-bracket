How to read this map

The tournament as one transit system. Every national team is a line in its own colors; every match is a station where two lines meet. Win and your service continues. Lose and it's the end of the line.

A team's line — hover to isolate its route, click to open its journey and ride the line.
Group-stage matchday results: win, draw, loss.
Terminus bar — the service ended here, labeled with the score that ended it.
Match station — the winner's flag sits at the core. Hover for details, click to pin them.
Under construction — the route exists, the traveler doesn't. Hover a future station to see every team that can still reach it.
The bronze shuttle — semifinal losers' spur to the third-place match.
The crown terminal at MetLife Stadium — reachable by exactly one line.

Left third: twelve group districts, four local lines each. The 32 qualifiers fan through the yard into the knockout trunk, where every station halves the network until one line remains.
